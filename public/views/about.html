About ianlamb.com

In the beginning

This began as a revamp of my old portfolio/personal website and a way to play with some new technologies. After some time working with angular and node I wanted to continue building stuff, so I started designing the adventure and gaming sections. These are things that really define me and I thought it would be a great way to showcase the kind of person I am as well as my development and design abilities.

The path ahead

I will continue to update and improve this website and essentially use it as a sandbox for trying new technologies. I am quite pleased with how it's turned out so far and really can't wait to share it with the world and use the knowledge I've gained to build other websites.
